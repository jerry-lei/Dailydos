Daily Dos is an online organizer site that allows you to neatly store your unfinished tasks.
To use the site, first make an account with a valid e-mail address. This will be the e-mail
address that you will use to manage your tasks. After creating your account, login at the
login page, and you will see your Task Manager. The Task Manager has a list of your
tasks, and an option to remove certain tasks. In order to add tasks to your Task Manager,
send an e-mail to group@stuy.edu, with the subject line being the task you want to add.
